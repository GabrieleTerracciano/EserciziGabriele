Focaccia with caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart, cranberries, anb creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!


Image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

INGREDIENTS

    For the focaccia:
  • 4 cups ( 500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 3/4 cups ( 350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/2 cup extra virgine olive oil, plus more for the pan
    For the topping:
  • 43 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not
    necessary)
  • 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-size mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix toghether all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
